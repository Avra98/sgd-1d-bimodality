The shape of SGD iterates in 2-D — sharp coordinate, tangent coordinate, and their coupling

The joint predictor model of Eq. (37): a sharp coordinate \(z\) at the edge of stability and a tangent coordinate \(y\) below it, coupled through \(\beta z^2 y\). Curvatures \(h_{z,t},h_{y,t}\) are redrawn every step from chosen laws. Drag \(\eta\) across the per-direction stability ladder and watch the joint quasi-stationary law go spike → broadened core → two lobes — or four, when both directions cross their edges.

$$z_{t+1}=z_t-\eta\Big[\big(h_{z,t}+2\beta y_t\big)z_t+\tfrac{c_{4z}}{6}z_t^{3}+\tfrac{c_{6z}}{120}z_t^{5}\Big]+\sigma_z\xi_t, \qquad y_{t+1}=y_t-\eta\Big[h_{y,t}\,y_t+\beta z_t^{2}+\tfrac{c_{4y}}{6}y_t^{3}+\tfrac{c_{6y}}{120}y_t^{5}\Big]+\sigma_y\xi'_t$$
Loss convention \(L=\tfrac{h_z}{2}z^2+\tfrac{c_{4z}}{24}z^4+\tfrac{c_{6z}}{720}z^6+\tfrac{h_y}{2}y^2+\tfrac{c_{4y}}{24}y^4+\tfrac{c_{6y}}{720}y^6+\beta z^2 y\). Sub-quartic \(c_{4z}<0\) drives the flip; the sextic term confines; the coupling feeds oscillation energy \(\nu^2+V\) into \(\bar y\), which feeds back into the effective sharpness \(h_z+2\beta\bar y\).

What “stability” means when d > 1

In one dimension the ladder is a family of scalar moment conditions \(\mathbb{E}|1-\eta h|^q<1\). In \(d>1\) the linear object is the random matrix cocycle \(\prod_t(I-\eta H_t)\), and the pathwise notion is the top Lyapunov exponent \(\lambda_{\mathrm{top}}\) (Eq. 9 of the draft): the origin is Lyapunov stable iff \(\lambda_{\mathrm{top}}<0\). The widget measures \(\lambda_{\mathrm{top}}\) live along a trajectory of the full nonlinear coupled system, Jacobian included coupling terms \(-2\eta\beta z\). The per-direction ladders shown above are the scalar ladders of the marginal cocycles — exact when \(\beta=0\) (block-diagonal \(H_t\)), and, when \(\beta\neq 0\), correct after replacing \(\mu_z\) by the effective curvature \(\mu_z+2\beta\bar y\): the slaved tangent mean shifts the sharp direction’s edges self-consistently (the “effective” row updates after each run). Bimodality remains a per-eigendirection statement — governed by the harmonic reinjection functional \(H(\eta)=\mathbb{E}|1-\eta h_{\mathrm{eff}}|^{-1}\) along that direction — while \(\lambda_{\mathrm{top}}\) only marks where the joint origin lets go. Crossing the ladder in one direction gives 2 lobes; in both directions (decouple with \(\beta\approx 0\)) gives 4.
