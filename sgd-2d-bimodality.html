The shape of SGD iterates in 2-D Sharp coordinate \(z\) with sampled curvature, coupled to a tangent coordinate \(y\) (Eq. 37 of the draft)

$$L_t=\tfrac{h_t}{2}z^2+\tfrac{c_4}{24}z^4+\tfrac{c_6}{6}z^6+\tfrac{g_t}{2}y^2+\beta z^2 y \;\;\Longrightarrow\;\; \begin{aligned} z_{t+1}&=z_t-\eta\big[(h_t+2\beta y_t)\,z_t+\tfrac{c_4}{6}z_t^3+c_6 z_t^5\big]+\sigma_z\xi_t\\ y_{t+1}&=y_t-\eta\big[g_t\,y_t+\beta z_t^2\big]+\sigma_y\xi'_t \end{aligned}$$

Both curvatures are redrawn each step: sharp \(h_t\) with mean \(\mu_z\), variance \(v_z\); tangent \(g_t\) with mean \(\mu_y\), variance \(v_y\). The coupling \(\beta z^2 y\) makes the effective sharp curvature \(h_t+2\beta y_t\): oscillation energy pushes \(\bar y\) negative, which feeds back into the multiplier distribution — a self-consistent Lyapunov edge instead of the fixed 1-D one. \(y\) is the representative tangent coordinate (\(\beta\) aggregates \(\sum_i\beta_i\)).

What each coefficient does
\(\eta\) — step size

Primary control. The bare edges \(\eta_{\mathrm{MS}}\), \(\eta_L\), \(2/\mu_z\) are computed from the law of \(h\) alone; the coupling shifts the live Lyapunov exponent \(\hat\chi_{\mathrm{eff}}\) below the bare \(\hat\chi\).

\(\mu_z, v_z\) and \(\mu_y, v_y\) — the two curvature laws

Spectral conditions of Eq. 37: \(\eta\mu_z\gtrless 2\) (sharp direction at/past the edge) and \(\eta\mu_y<2\) (tangent stable). Each direction can draw from a different law — that is the point of the 2-D lab.

\(\beta\) — cross coupling \(z^2y\)

Drives \(\bar y_\star=-\tfrac{\beta}{\mu_y}\,\mathbb{E}[z^2]\) (self-stabilization) and injects tangent fluctuations into the sharp multiplier. \(\beta=0\) decouples: the \(z\)-marginal reproduces the 1-D lab exactly.

\(c_4\) — quartic loss coefficient

\(c_4<0\): supercritical flip (\(D=-\mu_z c_4>0\)), small stable lobes. \(c_4>0\): subcritical in 1-D, but the draft predicts coupling can still sustain oscillation when \(2\beta^2/\mu_y>c_4/6\) — test it here.

\(c_6\) — sextic confinement

Outer wall (gradient term \(c_6 z^5\)). Keep \(c_6>0\) so ejected mass pools in lobes instead of escaping; the update is also clipped at \(|\Delta|\le\) clip, as in the multiplicative note.

\(\sigma_z,\ \sigma_y\) — additive noise

\(\sigma_z\) dresses the \(z\) lobes (additive theory). \(\sigma_y\) sets the tangent variance \(V_{y\star}=\sigma_y^2/[\eta\mu_y(2-\eta\mu_y)]\), which widens the sharp multiplier distribution through \(2\beta y\).
